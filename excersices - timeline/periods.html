Key Milestones in Graphic Design History

1440

Gutenberg’s Printing Press revolutionized book production and design.

1525

Albrecht Dürer developed the first grid system for design and composition.

1922

The term "Graphic Design" was first coined by William Addison Dwiggins.

1957

Helvetica Typeface was introduced, becoming a staple of modern design.

1984

Apple Macintosh transformed digital graphic design with user-friendly tools.

2000s

Adobe Creative Suite became the industry standard for graphic design.
